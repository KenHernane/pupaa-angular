FAQs

The PUP Appointment Assistant System is an online platform developed by the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) to facilitate the scheduling and management of appointments for various services offered by the university.

The system allows users to select the type of service they require, choose a preferred date and time slot, and make an appointment online. It ensures a streamlined and efficient process for both users and the university staff.

The PUP Appointment Assistant System enables users to book appointments for services such as admissions, enrollment, counseling, library access, academic advising, and more. The available services may vary based on the university's offerings.

No, you do not need to create an account in order to use the system. The system allows you to manage your appointments, receive notifications, and access additional features provided by the system for free.

Yes, the PUP Appointment Assistant System displays the available appointment slots based on the selected service and preferred date. You can choose from the displayed options according to your convenience.

The availability of appointment slots may vary depending on the service you are booking. Generally, the system allows users to book appointments within a specified timeframe, which could range from a few days to several weeks in advance.

The PUP Appointment Assistant System is accessible online 24/7, allowing users to make appointments at their convenience. However, the availability of specific services and appointment slots may vary based on the university's operating hours and policies.

Yes, upon successful booking of an appointment, the PUP Appointment Assistant System will generate a confirmation message or email containing the details of your appointment. It is advisable to keep this confirmation for reference or future communication with the university.

Note that missed appointments may result in a delay or rescheduling of your desired service.
